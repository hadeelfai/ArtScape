About Us

Feature Description
Mission Statement At ArtSpace, our mission is to celebrate and promote the vibrant voices of local and emerging artists. We aim to provide a dynamic platform where art can inspire, provoke, and connect with the community.
Our History Founded in 2023, ArtSpace began as a passion project born from a love for the arts and a desire to showcase unique talents. Our founder, Hadeel Alharthi, envisioned a space that would bridge the gap between artists and the community, allowing for deeper engagement and appreciation of contemporary art.
Art Focus At ArtSpace, we specialize in contemporary art, featuring a diverse range of mediums, including painting, sculpture, photography, and mixed media.
Future Plans Looking ahead, we are excited to announce upcoming exhibitions featuring the renowned artist Vincent van Gogh, showcasing his unique style and profound impact on the Impressionist movement.
Community Programs Expansion We are committed to enriching our community engagement initiatives, including launching a “Youth Art Mentorship Program” where local artists will mentor young creatives.
Community Engagement We believe that art is a powerful tool for community building. Our gallery regularly hosts workshops, artist talks, and educational programs aimed at engaging the public and fostering a deeper understanding of art.
Exhibition Space Located in Jeddah Obhur, our gallery features a spacious layout with natural light and unique architectural features.
Contact Us We’d love to hear from you! Feel free to reach out to us at ArtScape@gmail.com.
